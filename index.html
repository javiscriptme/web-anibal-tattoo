About Antiek

ANTIEK TATTOO is a gallery and tattoo shop located in Rozengracht 148, in the heart of Amsterdam. Close to the Anna Frank House and in the same place Rembrandt had his last studio and residence. Because of this, we, all the artists in Antiek Tattoo have a tremendous pride of work here.

Pass by our place to check artworks from our team and other talented international artists. We have high quality hand-made tattoo machines and merchandising. And, of course, we would love to help you with your next custom tattoo.

— Anibal Fernandez
