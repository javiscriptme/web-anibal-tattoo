About Antiek

The shop is located in the street Rozengracht, number 148, in the center of Amsterdam. In this same place Rembrandt had his last studio and residence. Because of this, all the artists and me that are working in Antiek Tattoo we have a tremendous pride of being doing here an activity related to art.

I invite you to visit the studio to enjoy the art gallery that counts with works of international artists, an excelent selection of japanese antiques and where you can see the artists working in an enviroment in which you can breathe art.

In Antiek Tattoo we listen to our customers and we make original works for each and every one of them. We are waiting for you.

— Anibal Fernandez
